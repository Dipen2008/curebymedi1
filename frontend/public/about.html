Our story

CureByMedi started as a personal frustration. Walking out of a chemist with a strip of pills, the only info we had was a tiny printed label — and Google didn't always help. Indian medicine names, compositions, and brand info aren't easy to find in one place that a normal person can understand.

So we built it: a free, no-login-required medicine encyclopaedia for India. Search any name, see composition / what it's used for / daily dosage / side effects / how to take. Snap a photo and an AI reads the strip for you. Check if two medicines are safe together. Set pill reminders so you never miss a dose.

We are not doctors, this is not a substitute for medical advice, and we are not responsible for any decision you take based on this information. We're just trying to make medicine info less mysterious — the rest is up to you and your doctor.
