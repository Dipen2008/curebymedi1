Privacy policy

Last updated: January 2026

What we store

  • Account: your email and a one-way bcrypt-hashed password (we cannot read your password).
  • Favorites / recently viewed / reminders: the medicines you save and the times you set.
  • AI scans: we keep the AI-detected medicine name and a short summary for the admin audit log. We do not permanently store the photo you upload — it is sent to the AI provider, analysed, and discarded. Scan records older than 90 days are automatically deleted.
  • Language preference: English / Hindi.

What we never store

  • Your real name (we only know your email).
  • Payment information — we do not charge for the service.
  • Medical records, prescriptions, or chat content.
  • Your phone number, address, or location.

Who else sees your data

  • AI provider (OpenAI): when you scan a photo or ask for interactions / symptoms, the text/image is sent to the AI provider to generate a response. They have their own privacy policy.
  • Hosting: our cloud host (MongoDB Atlas + Render) stores the database — encrypted at rest.
  • We never sell or share your data with advertisers.

Your rights

You can delete your entire account (and all favorites / reminders) anytime from your profile → Delete my account. This is permanent and immediate.
